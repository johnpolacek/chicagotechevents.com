WiDS Chicago is an independent event organized to coincide with the annual Global Women in Data Science (WiDS) Conference held at Stanford University and an estimated 150+ locations worldwide. All genders are invited to attend WiDS regional events, which features outstanding women doing outstanding work. This year, WiDS: Chicago will feature entirely local, live talks from female data scientists in both industry and academia, with particular focus on collaborative tools for data science and ethics in data science.
